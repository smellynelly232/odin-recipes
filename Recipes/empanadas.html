Empanadas De Queso

Description

The Venezuelan Arepa is made using corn flour dough filled with anything you want.

They are different from other empanadas that are flakier

Ingredients

  • Arina Pan (corn flour)
  • Large Tupperware/Bowl
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Queso Fresco (White Shredded Cheese)
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Fill a large Tupperware or Bowl 3 quarters of the way with water
  2. Pour corn flour until it is up past the water
  3. Add salt and a little bit of sugar
  4. Mix the flour until it is doughy and holds a shape on its own
  5. roll dough into a ball and flatten it into as even a circle as you can onto a gallon ziploc bag or empanada folder
  6. smear butter on dough and drop a small mound of cheese on the center
  7. Use the ziploc bag to fold the flat circle in half any direction
  8. use a cereal bowl to cut out the curved shape of the empanada through the bag
  9. Fry in a pan with oil high enough to cover half of each side of the empanada
  10. Cook evenly and let cool
