Sausage Rolls

Description

Sausage rolls are an awesome breakfast food

It is super easy to make and uses few ingredients

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Cream Cheese
  • Crescent Rolls

Steps

  1. Cook your Sausage
  2. Drain Oil and add Cream Cheese
  3. Lay out all your Crescent Rolls
  4. Spread the Cream Cheese Sausage onto the Rolls
  5. Roll them up and bake at 350F for 15 minutes
